Tonight's Reflection

Questions for the room

This data isn't about compliance. It's about building a practice you can see, coach, and grow.

  • 01 If you entered data — what did the act of writing down someone's self-interest teach you about the conversation?
  • 02 If you haven't entered data yet — was the barrier the tool, the time, or the uncertainty about who counts?
  • 03 Look at the names on your list. Which of them could you invite to take a specific action this week?
  • 04 What's one 1-on-1 you could do between now and next week's session? Name the person.
Week 2 goal: every fellow enters at least one 1-on-1 with notes + self-interest
The spreadsheet is not the point. The practice is. The spreadsheet just makes the practice visible.
