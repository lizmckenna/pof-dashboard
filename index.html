Tonight's Reflection

Questions for the room

This data isn't about compliance. It's about building a practice you can see, coach, and grow.

  • 01 Look at someone whose self-interest you wrote down. What did the act of writing it down teach you about the conversation you actually had?
  • 02 177 prospects listed, 37 done, 10 scheduled. What's the gap for you — calendar, courage, or unclear who counts?
  • 03 Look at the names on your list. Which of them could you invite to take a specific action this week?
  • 04 What's one 1-on-1 you could do between now and next week's session? Name the person.
The spreadsheet just makes the practice visible.
